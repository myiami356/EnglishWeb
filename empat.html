Slang words you often see on social media!

Teen slangs are slang words or phrases that are often used by teenagers, especially on social media such as TikTok, Instagram, or Snapchat. These words usually have a different meaning from their literal meaning and can change with trends.

This slang reflects pop culture, emotions, or reactions in a short and expressive way. Although not always used in formal situations, understanding slang is important so that we don't get left behind and can understand everyday conversations.
